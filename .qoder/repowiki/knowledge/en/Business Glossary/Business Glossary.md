### Planejamento
- Definition：Portuguese term meaning 'planning' or 'schedule' — the core business domain of this academic scheduling application. Refers to course planning, timetable management, and academic resource allocation.
- Aliases：planejamentos、planejamento5

### Docente
- Definition：Portuguese term for 'academic staff' or 'faculty member' — represents teachers/professors in the academic scheduling system who have availability constraints and teach disciplines.
- Aliases：docentes

### Disciplina
- Definition：Portuguese term for 'course' or 'subject' — represents academic courses offered by the institution, including curriculum information and teaching assignments.
- Aliases：disciplinas

### Sala
- Definition：Portuguese term for 'classroom' or 'room' — represents physical spaces available for academic activities, with capacity and equipment constraints.
- Aliases：salas

### Horário
- Definition：Portuguese term for 'time slot' or 'schedule period' — represents specific time periods for academic activities, used for room and instructor scheduling.
- Aliases：horarios

### Ementa
- Definition：Portuguese term for 'course syllabus' or 'curriculum outline' — contains the detailed content description and learning objectives for each discipline.
- Aliases：ementas

### Optativa
- Definition：Portuguese term for 'elective course' — represents optional courses that students can choose from as part of their academic program requirements.
- Aliases：optativas

### Usuário Planejamento
- Definition：Portuguese term for 'user planning relationship' — represents the association between users and their planning responsibilities or access permissions within the scheduling system.
- Aliases：usuarioplanejamentos
